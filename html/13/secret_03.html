This secret helps you memorize all the different readings of Japanese numbers.

The most enjoyable way to do this is to decipher a range of genuine Tokyo business phone-numbers. In Japan telephone numbers can actually be "read," thus a butcher's shop might have a number that reads "Good Meat" (orYoi niku in Japanese), while a body odor clinic might have a number reading "Smell! Go away!" (Sare yo nioi).

Three stages lead up to a quiz in which Sherlock Holmes challenges you to make observations and deductions about a suspect ("Mr. X") based only on "reading" the Japanese phone-numbers in his address book.
