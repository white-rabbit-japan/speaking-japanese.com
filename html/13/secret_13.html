This secret is designed to make you aware of the benefits of comicbooks as a Japanese-language study tool.

Tezuka Productions very kindly gave us permission to reprint part of a story from the BlackJack series by Osamu Tezuka.

Tezuka - sometimes called "The God of Comics" - is Japan's most revered manga artist. The manga is printed here with a translation on the facing page and with comprehensive vocabulary lists.
