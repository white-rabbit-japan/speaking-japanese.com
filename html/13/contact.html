How to find the book in Japan.
13 Secrets for Speaking Fluent Japanese costs ¥1900. It is published by Kodansha International, andthe Japanese title is 'Nihongo o Perapera Hanasu tame no Ju-san no Hiketsu.' It is available in the foreign book section of major bookstore chains such as KINOKUNIYA, MARUZEN & TAISEIDO, in specialist educational bookstores, and in bookstores in areas with a large foreign population such as Aoyama Book Center (Roppongi, Tokyo), the Oriental Bazaar (Omote-Sando,Tokyo), or Tower Records (Shibuya, Tokyo). You may be also able to find it in airports and hotels! Remember, if you can't get your hands on it, you can order it from the bookstore. If that proves difficult then call the Kodansha International Sales Department in Tokyo at (03)-3944-6492 and ask for their advice.

How to find the book in America and other countries.
