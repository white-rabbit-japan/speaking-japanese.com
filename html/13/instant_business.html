 Most books on business Japanese only teach very stiff, proper and formal language. But Instant Business Japanese-as its slogan 'Real Life Skills for Real-life Situations' proclaims- is designed to make you into something more than a stuttering stuffed shirt, by teaching you how to be articulate and persuasive.
Useful expressions are taught through example sentences, and a dialogue-based story that features a couple of marketing consultants relaunching a word-processor manufacturer as a maker of cool, multimedia PCs. Humorous notes also explain some of the quirkier Japanese business practises.
Praised by the Financial Times as 'a down to earth insight into Japanese business' and by the Asahi Evening News for teaching 'expressions not usually taught in language schools,' Instant Business Japanese will enable you to function as a fully versatile businessperson in a Japanese environment.
